Job Summary

We are seeking an enthusiastic and forward-thinking student to drive hands-on projects that help implement AI best practices across our organization. This role is ideal for someone passionate about artificial intelligence, operational excellence, and solving real business problems with practical, scalable solutions.

The AI Integration intern will design and implement a standardized, scalable datasheet framework across all IntelliPower products. The intern will lead the effort to audit existing datasheets, identify specification inconsistencies and errors, and migrate all content into a unified, current template.

The project will leverage AI-assisted tools, engineering validation, and marketing alignment to ensure all datasheets are technically accurate, visually consistent, and market-ready. The standardized datasheet system will improve data integrity, reduce internal confusion, and enhance customer trust while enabling faster updates going forward.

Key Responsibilities

Lead an end-to-end‑ project focused on implementing the best AI practice to deliver a standardized datasheet template aligned across all product lines.
Research industry frameworks, emerging AI trends, and relevant best practices to recommend ongoing AI- assisted content maintenance and version control.
Document solutions, workflows, and technical designs to support knowledge transfer and sustainability.
Collaborate with stakeholders across functions to gather requirements, understand current processes, and define clear success metrics.
Develop documentation outlining datasheet standards, governance, and updated processes.
Build simple prototypes, process maps, or proof‑of‑concept demonstrations as appropriate.
Implement AI Agents or other productivity tools that drive simplification, efficiency, and speed.
Present final project outcomes, impact, and recommendations to business and technical leaders.
