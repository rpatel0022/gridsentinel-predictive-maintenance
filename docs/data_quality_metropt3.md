# MetroPT-3 data-quality spike — report

_Generated by `python -m pipelines.data_quality` on the full UCI #791 CSV
(`MetroPT3(AirCompressor).csv`, MD5 of the published Zenodo 7766691 archive).
Re-run it whenever the dataset or `pipelines/metropt3_schema.py` changes._

This is the Phase 0 spike that ADR-0001 requires before any modeling: it confirms
MetroPT-3 is usable and turns the schema's previously-`TBC` physical ranges into
values backed by the real distribution.

tightened from `TBC` to the bounds below.

## Analog channels — observed extremes → schema bound

Bounds bracket the observed min/max with a physical margin: wide enough to admit a
plausible unseen operating point (e.g. a colder winter oil temperature than the
Feb–Sep sample saw), tight enough to reject genuine corruption (a stuck `9999`
line, a sign-flipped channel). All 1,516,948 rows fall inside every bound.

Small negative pressures/current (e.g. -0.036) are sensor noise around zero, so
the lower bounds stay slightly negative rather than clamping at 0.
